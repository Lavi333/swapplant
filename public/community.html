SWAPPLANT

At Swapplant, we believe that plants have the power to transform spaces and bring joy to people's lives.

Our mission is to connect like-minded individuals who are passionate about plants and to make it easy for them to share and trade their green friends.We also believe in the importance of sustainability, and we want to encourage the re-use and sharing of plants rather than purchasing new ones.

Browse and search for plants that you would like to trade
Connect with other users to arrange a trade
Meet up in person or ship plants to each other
